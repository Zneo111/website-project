Jaguar F-PACE SVR. The Ultimate Performance SUV

The Jaguar F-PACE SVR is a high-performance SUV that blends British luxury with raw power. Jaguar’s Special Vehicle Operations (SVO) division took the standard F-PACE and injected it with supercharged aggression.

Styling. Sleek Yet Aggressive

The F-PACE SVR features a muscular stance, with a vented hood and larger air intakes to enhance cooling. The quad exhaust setup at the rear isn’t just for show—it delivers an intoxicating V8 roar. The 22-inch forged alloy wheels and optional blacked-out styling pack add to its sporty appeal.

Interior

Step inside, and you’re greeted by premium Windsor leather seats with suede inserts and embossed SVR badging. The Pivi Pro infotainment system, with a 12.3-inch digital driver’s display, keeps you connected. Aluminium paddle shifters and carbon-fiber trim add to the race-inspired feel.

Power & Performance

Under the hood, a 5.0L Supercharged V8 pushes out 575 hp and 700 Nm of torque. Power is sent to all four wheels via an 8-speed ZF automatic transmission. The result? 0-100 km/h in just 4.0 seconds, making it one of the fastest SUVs on the road. The tone of the V8 beats all other competitors including BMW especially the exhaust sounds. Verdict: If you want an SUV with both brutal performance and daily usability, the F-PACE SVR is a solid choice.
