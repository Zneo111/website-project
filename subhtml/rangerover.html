Range Rover Sport SV. Power & Prestige Combined

The Range Rover Sport SV redefines what a luxury performance SUV should be. It’s sleek, powerful, and packed with tech, making it a perfect blend of off-road capability and street dominance.

Elegant but Dominating

With a bold new front grille, slim LED headlights, and aerodynamic enhancements, the Sport SV commands attention. The 23-inch alloy wheels and an optional carbon-fiber hood add a sporty touch.

The Pinnacle of Comfort

Inside, you’ll find ultra-luxurious leather seats, a minimalist dashboard, and a 13.1-inch curved touchscreen. The Meridian Signature Sound System delivers an immersive audio experience, while massaging seats ensure total comfort.

Power & Performance

The heart of the SV is a 4.4L twin-turbo V8, producing 626 hp and 800 Nm of torque. Simmilar engines to what you'll find in the X5 M competition (s68) Paired with an 8-speed automatic, it rockets from 0-100 km/h in just 3.8 seconds. The new 6D Dynamic suspension keeps the ride smooth and composed.
