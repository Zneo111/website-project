The World’s First Mega-GT

The Koenigsegg Gemera is a four-seater hypercar that blends mind-blowing speed with practicality.

Futuristic & Bold

With dihedral synchro-helix doors, a wide stance, and aerodynamic bodywork, the Gemera looks like something from another planet.

Hypercar Meets Grand Tourer

Unlike traditional hypercars, the Gemera has four luxury seats, a high-tech infotainment system, and even cupholders—a rarity in this segment.

Insane Hybrid Power

With a 2.0L twin-turbo 3-cylinder engine and three electric motors, the Gemera produces a whopping 1,700 hp and 3,500 Nm of torque. 0-100 km/h in 1.9 seconds. The itteration also includes a Koenigsegg hot V8 5l twin-turbo coupled with 3 e-motors to produce; 2300 hp and 2750 Nm
