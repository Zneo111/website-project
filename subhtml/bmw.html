BMW X5 M Competition. The M-Powered SUV King

The BMW X5 M Competition isn’t just a luxury SUV, it’s a track-ready monster with brutal acceleration and razor-sharp handling.

Styling, Bold and Aggressive

The massive kidney grille, M-specific body kit, and quad exhaust pipes make the X5 M Competition stand out. 21-inch front and 22-inch rear wheels add to its muscular stance, while the aggressive rear diffuser screams performance.

Interior. A Tech-Filled Performance Cabin

Inside, you get M Sport bucket seats wrapped in Merino leather, along with carbon-fiber trim. The 12.3-inch curved display with BMW’s iDrive 8 system offers seamless connectivity, while the head-up display keeps you focused on the road.

Power & Performance

The S68 4.4L twin-turbo V8 cranks out 617 hp and 750 Nm of torque. Power is sent to all four wheels via an M-tuned xDrive system, allowing for 0-100 km/h in just 3.7 seconds. The adaptive M suspension ensures precise handling, whether on the track or the highway.

Verdict: If you crave performance in an SUV without sacrificing luxury, the X5 M Competition is an absolute beast.
