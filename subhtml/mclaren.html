The Hardcore Supercar

The McLaren 765LT is built for speed, precision, and pure driving thrill. It’s lighter, faster, and more aggressive than anything McLaren has produced before.

Aerodynamic and Race-Ready

From the massive rear wing to the functional air vents, every design element on the 765LT serves a performance purpose. The ultra-lightweight carbon-fiber body and front splitter improve downforce.

Interior

Stripped Down for Speed. The 765LT’s cabin is all about weight reduction. It features racing bucket seats, Alcantara-wrapped surfaces, and a minimalistic dashboard. There’s no excess luxury—just raw performance.

Power & Performance

With a 4.0L twin-turbo V8, the 765LT produces 755 hp (765 ps) and 800 Nm of torque transmitted to the rear wheels only via a 7-speed dual clutch transmission, and weighing just 1,229 kg. It launches from 0-100 km/h in 2.8 seconds and hits 200 km/h in 7.0 seconds.

Verdict: If you want one of the fastest and most exhilarating supercars ever built, the 765LT is a no-brainer.
